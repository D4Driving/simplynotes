My Story

Teacher, Composer
& Published Author

The SimplyNotes project was created to assist parents and teachers of children learning the piano. As a practising teacher, I create musical miniatures, simple etudes, and short musical narratives that keep students engaged and motivated during their practice sessions.

Graduated from the State Music School in Poland, I have been living and teaching in the UK since 2006 — working with students of all ages across piano, guitar, ukulele and xylophone.
