Frequently asked questions

How do I receive my PDF after purchase?

You'll receive an email with a download link within a few minutes of payment. Check your spam folder if you don't see it. Links are valid for 48 hours.

Can I print the sheet music?

Yes — all PDFs are licensed for personal and teaching use. You may print as many copies as you need for your own students.

What is your refund policy?

Because these are digital downloads, we generally cannot offer refunds once a file has been delivered. If you have a problem, please contact us and we'll do our best to help.

Can I use these pieces for recitals?

Yes — Joanna's original compositions are free to perform at private recitals, school concerts and examinations. Please credit "Joanna Bernat / SimplyNotes" in programmes.

I lost my download link — what do I do?

Just drop us a message using the contact form above with your order email address and we'll resend it promptly.

Are the PDF books the same as the Amazon paperbacks?

Yes — the content is identical. PDFs are the instant-download digital version; Amazon paperbacks are the printed physical edition. Choose whichever suits you best.
